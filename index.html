About Us

Empowering Futures Through Quality Education

NEx-gEN School of Computers has been a premier computer training institute in Srikakulam since 2007. We are proud to hold the ISO 9001:2015 certification for providing quality software, hardware training & IT solutions.

With experienced faculty, air-conditioned labs, and hands-on practical sessions, we prepare students for real-world IT careers. Our methodology: Theory + Practicals + Assignment + Evaluation = Certificate.

  • Estd. 2007 – 18+ years of trust
  • ISO 9001:2015 Certified Institute

  • Lifetime support & career guidance
